Who said E-Sports are only for humans?

Well OK, machines have a better reaction time and have access to all the knowledge humanity accumulated for centuries. But what machine cannot do, is to defeat us tactically. We, humans with our ingenuity could defeate machine any time, given the "messy and continuous nature of the real world." We can navigate the world better and find loopholes in it and plan accordingly. And what is more messy, continuous and unpredictable than a Dota 2 match?

OpenAI Fife not that long ago proved that machine could triumph over the man even in a messy and unpredictable world of Dota 2
